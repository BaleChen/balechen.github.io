Hi! I am a second-year CS PhD student jointly at [NYU Courant](https://cims.nyu.edu/dynamic/) and [NYU Shanghai](https://shanghai.nyu.edu), mentored by Professor [Chen Zhao](http://www.chenz.umiacs.io/). I also closely work with Professor [Xi Ye](https://xiye17.github.io/) at University of Alberta. Previously, I received my B.S. degree in Data Science (with Honors) from New York University Shanghai, where I was advised by Professor [Wilson Tam](https://shanghai.nyu.edu/academics/faculty/directory/yik-cheung-wilson-tam) and Professor [Mathieu Lauriere](https://mlauriere.github.io/). My primary research interests are in Natural Language Processing and Deep Learning. Here are some topics that I have been thinking about lately:

1. How to best empower LLMs with the information retrieval ability, so that they can reliably solve complex real-world agentic tasks (e.g. Deep Research report generation) that involves knowledge-intensive reasoning? How can (Deep) Research Agents best collaborate and interact with human researchers?
2. Can we automate the [error characterization](http://duboue.net/blog37.html) process of agentic traces, or long-form generation in general? Given a set of generations, can LLM accurately induce and summarize what the error patterns are?
3. I am always fascinated by novel and smart decoding strategies and embedding learning methods!

If you have any thoughts on these, shoot me an email and I am more than happy to chat with you!
